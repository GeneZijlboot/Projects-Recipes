Gene's Pasta Pesto Recipe

Ingredients

  • 300 gram pasta
  • 200 ml cooking cream
  • 190 gram pesto
  • 2 garlic gloves
  • salt & pepper
  • a few fresh basil leaves
  • a tablespoon parmesan chease
  • 30 gram pine nuts

Steps

  1. heat up 500ml water
  2. when the water starts boiling add the pasta (look at the package how long you're pasta should cook)
  3. heat up the pan
  4. put olive oil in the pan
  5. roast the pine nuts
  6. add the cooked pasta with the pesto and the coocking cream
  7. next put in some salt and pepper
  8. put you're Pasta Pesto on a plate and add parmesan chease with some fresh basil
