Gene's Nasi Recipe

Ingredients

  • 2 cups rise
  • 3 cups water
  • 2 chicken breasts
  • 2 eggs
  • sping onion

spices

  • 2 teaspoons of cumin powder
  • 1 teaspoon of turmeric
  • 1 teaspoon of ginger powder
  • 2 teaspoons of ground coriander
  • 2 teaspoons of galangal

Steps

  1. heat up the 3 cups of water
  2. once it starts boiling put the rise in and let it boil for 8-10 minutes
  3. when its done get the pan of the stove cut up the chicken into small cubes
  4. and cook them in a pan
  5. on the side also cook the 2 eggs
  6. when the chicken and eggs are ready
  7. put the rice and chicken in a pan and put all the spices (given above)
  8. when all the spices have mixed in, cut up the egg in small pices and put it in the nasi
  9. then cut up some small spring onion pieces and put them in the nasi and its ready
