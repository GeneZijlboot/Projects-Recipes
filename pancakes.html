Gene's Pancake Recipe

Ingredients

  • 100 gram plain flour
  • 300 ml milk
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tablespoon oil

Steps

  1. put a olive oil in the pan
  2. pour the pancake mix in the pan
  3. now wait till the bottem is crispy golden brown and flip!
  4. finally add some blueberrys and maple syrup
